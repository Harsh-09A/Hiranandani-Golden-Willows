About HIRANANDANI Group

Established in 1978, Hiranandani Group is one of the most prestigious real estate developers based in Mumbai. Hiranandani Group has the vision of creating a new India with self-sufficient and enduring townships. Every activity, be it in real estate, education, healthcare, hospitality, leisure or entertainment has steadily focused on creating a better experience in every aspect of life.

While corporate has gone from strength to strength, the Group is primarily synonymous with quality, commitment towards customers, reliability, and excellence in architecture. We are here to give an experience of a lifetime to every family, through infrastructure that is synonymous with supreme quality, beauty, and longevity.
